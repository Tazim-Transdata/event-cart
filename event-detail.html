Join us on an adventurous trip to Lapland Finland 200 km North from the Arctic Circle. Lapland offers many lovely memories for everyone. Experience winter twilight, Northern Lights and BRRRRRR! cold temperatures.

Along with unique nature, become revitalized with a huge range of activities. Transportation, cottage stay + Basic breakfast in Lapland and sauna is included in the price.

Pickup Places.

  • Vaasa (Linna building Palosaarentie 79)
  • Kokkola Bus Station
  • Oulu Train Station.
